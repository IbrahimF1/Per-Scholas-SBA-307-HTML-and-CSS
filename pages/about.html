About Me

I am a Finnish-American software engineer, best known as the creator and, for a long time, the principal developer of the Linux kernel. I also created the distributed version control system Git.

I was born in Helsinki, Finland. My interest in computers began with a Commodore VIC-20. I approach programming with a pragmatic philosophy: "Talk is cheap. Show me the code."
